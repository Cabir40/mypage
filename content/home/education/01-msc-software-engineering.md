Investigated sentence-aware document splitting algorithms and systematic
parameter optimization (Split Length, Top-K, Window Size) for RAG-powered
healthcare LLM assistants. Demonstrated that sentence-aware splitting
significantly outperforms character-based methods, improving retrieval
quality in clinical document analysis.
